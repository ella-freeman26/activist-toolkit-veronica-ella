Education for Africa
We advocate equal opportunities of education for children in Africa!

Why there is a lack of eductation in Africa:

There are only six years of required education in Africa making many families opt for not continuing schools as a way to save money. This lack of money and resources makes it impossible for children to get an education and support their family at the same time.

Image taken from Pixabay.

Current Education in Africa

Many children do not attend school,for one third of the children ages 12-14 are out of school. The current education system is understaffed and underfunded, so many of the children that do attend do not have school supplies. Out of the children that attend, majority are male, as females are needed to help their family/home situation.

Image taken from Pixabay.

Effects of Education:

Education improves the lives of all students by decreasing the spread of disease, teaching agricultural techniques which helps secure a stable food supply, and developing girls to understand their bodies.

Why you should take action:

The children, their families, and future generations in Africa are all impacted by affordable education. With the knowledge the children learn in school, they will be able to grow and support their family and their future family which in turn will better their community.
